Option 1 · HSR + TRA

The fastest option. Take a short shuttle to Taoyuan HSR Station, ride the High Speed Rail to Taichung, then transfer indoors to a TRA local train.

⭐

Top Recommendation — Fastest route with clear English signage at every transfer. Ideal for participants carrying luggage.

EasyCard · 悠遊卡

Taiwan's universal transit smart card. Works like an Oyster Card or IC Card — tap in, tap out. Essential for TRA local trains and city buses in Taichung.

Where to Buy

You can get an EasyCard right after landing at Taoyuan Airport.

🏦

At the Airport (Recommended)

Go to the EZFly counter (桃園機場捷運服務台) on the B1 level near the Airport MRT gates. Price: NT$300 total (NT$100 deposit + NT$200 stored value). Credit card accepted — this is one of the few places where you can buy with a card.

🚇

At MRT Station Machines

All MRT station ticket machines sell EasyCards. Cost: NT$100 deposit (non-refundable) + any stored value. Add value with cash at machines or service windows.

🏪

Convenience Stores

7-Eleven, FamilyMart, Hi-Life, and OK Mart all sell EasyCards. There are convenience stores inside every train station in Taiwan.

📱

Pre-order via Klook

Order online before you arrive and pick up at the Klook kiosk in the arrivals hall (open 04:30–23:00). Pay by credit card in advance.

How to Use

Purchase the card. The NT$100 deposit is non-refundable. Any remaining stored value can be refunded when you leave Taiwan.

Add value (儲值) at any MRT station add-value machine or at a convenience store cashier. Simply tap the card on the reader and add cash. No registration required.

At transit gates, tap the card firmly on the yellow sensor pad to enter and tap again to exit. The fare is automatically deducted.

Your balance is shown on a small screen after each tap. Top up whenever you run low — machines and convenience stores across Taiwan can top you up in seconds.
